Viking Rise

If you're a fan of strategy games, Viking Rise is the ultimate choice for you. As a Viking leader, you'll embark on a journey to explore, plunder, and conquer the world of Midgard.

Viking Rise offers a unique blend of strategy, exploration, and real-time warfare. You'll lead your Viking Tribe through the uncharted realms of Valhalla, where magnificent oceans and towering mountains await your conquest.


But your journey in Viking Rise is not just about battles; it's also about forming alliances with players from around the world. Viking Rise offers global multiplayer battles, allowing you to prove your strategic skills and establish your Viking empire in Valhalla.

Design your territory in Viking Rise and personalize it according to your vision. Create a powerful military fortress or a thriving trading center; the choice is yours.

In the heart of Viking Rise, naval combat plays a crucial role. Master the seas, carry out surprise attacks, and outmaneuver your opponents to claim strategic territories.

Real-time combat in Viking Rise ensures that you'll face challenges on land and at sea. With a legion of Viking Heroes at your side, including Ragnar, Bjorn, and Ivar the Boneless, you'll lead your Tribe to victory.

But don't forget the mythical beasts that roam Midgard. Send your Heroes to hunt down these creatures, tame a powerful dragon, and become a legend in Viking Rise.


Embrace the adventure, answer the call of Valhalla, and experience the epic world-building of Viking Rise.
