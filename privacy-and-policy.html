Privacy Policy

Last updated: [11/18/2023]

Welcome to OlitechGames. This Privacy Policy outlines how OlitechGames collects, utilizes, and safeguards information when you engage with our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect a range of personal information, such as your name, email address, and other relevant details, when you register on our website, make a purchase, or subscribe to our newsletter. Additionally, we may gather non-personal information, including browser details, IP address, and website interactions for analytical purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We leverage the information we collect for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders
  • Providing personalized customer support
  • Enhancing our products and services
  • Delivering promotional emails and updates (you can opt-out at any time)

Third-Party Services

To optimize our services and better understand user behavior, we may integrate third-party services. These services maintain their privacy policies that govern the use of your information. Specifically, our website employs Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google. AdSense employs cookies to display ads based on your visits to our site and other websites. You have the option to opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings page.

Our Advertising Partners

Some of the advertisers on OlitechGames may utilize cookies and web beacons for various purposes. Please review the Privacy Policies of our advertising partners listed below for details on their data practices:

  • Google: Google Privacy Policy

Each advertising partner operates under its own Privacy Policy, and we encourage you to visit the provided links for easier access to their specific policies regarding user data.

Privacy Regulations

Our privacy practices align with global privacy regulations, including but not limited to:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us at [contact@olitechgames.com].
  • CPRA (California Privacy Rights Act): If you are a resident of California, USA, you have the right to request information about the collection of your personal information, opt-out of the sale of personal information, and request the deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at [contact@olitechgames.com].

Security Measures

We prioritize the security of your personal information. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

Your Choices

You have the right to review, update, or delete the personal information we have collected. If you wish to exercise these rights or have any inquiries regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at [contact@olitechgames.com].

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any updates will be effective upon posting the revised policy on this page. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
