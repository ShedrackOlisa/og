Shadow Slayer Demon Hunter

Explore the world of Shadow Slayer, an action RPG that combines thrilling anime-style combat with the excitement of a hack and slash game. Join the heroes of Foliga in their quest to reclaim their once peaceful world, now overrun by undead bosses. Arm yourself with epic hero powers and embark on the battle of a lifetime!


Engage in Hardcore Action

Shadow Slayer offers a hardcore hack-and-slash action-RPG experience. Prepare to enter the dark and challenging world of Foliga, where undead bosses have taken control.

The time for action is now! Unlock your hidden potential, acquire new skills, and prepare to face menacing monsters in this exhilarating adventure.

Battle Fierce Enemies

Face a multitude of diverse monsters and powerful bosses within the game's dungeons. Train and equip yourself to challenge these formidable foes and showcase your combat prowess. The path to becoming a legendary hero begins with each battle!

Epic Boss Battles

Prepare for unforgettable encounters with massive, bloodthirsty bosses. To emerge victorious, you'll need not only powerful equipment but also superior combat skills. The game's massive boss battles will test your mettle and strategy, providing an epic challenge.

Play as Multiple Characters

Enjoy the flexibility of playing as multiple unique characters, each with their own skills, gameplay style, and assets. With distinct approaches to strategy and combat, every character offers a fresh gaming experience. Choose your character and embark on the adventure that suits your style!

Uncover Hidden Treasures

The game is filled with mysterious treasure chests, waiting to be discovered. Sharpen your observation skills to ensure you don't miss valuable rewards hidden throughout the game. These treasures will aid you on your journey to becoming the ultimate hero.

Play Anytime, Anywhere

Shadow Slayer provides the convenience of playing at your own pace, anytime and anywhere, even in offline mode. Dive into the action and confront the undead bosses of Foliga wherever you are.
