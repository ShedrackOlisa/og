World of Kings

Prepare to embark on an epic adventure in the fantastical realm of World of Kings, a 3D MMORPG masterpiece that seamlessly blends stunning graphics with classic gameplay. This immersive world offers players a sprawling open landscape filled with wonders and challenges.


What's New in the Latest Update

The latest update brings a wave of exciting content to World of Kings:

  1. Open the new Racing Dungeon-Icyfire Ridge, offering a thrilling new challenge.
  2. Introducing the new Trial—Nightmare Dread, for those who seek greater challenges.
  3. The coveted Tainted T4 set is now open, attainable by brave warriors participating in event trials like Nightmare Dread and Guild Dungeon: Song of Tides.
  4. Discover the all-new Fallen Heroes Temple "Harma Temple" and a new Fallen Heroes Race, the "Orc," expanding the world of possibilities.
An Immersive Fantasy World

In this massive open world, spanning over 26 square kilometers, you have the freedom to craft your character as you see fit.

Choose from six distinct races and a staggering 30 advanced classes, setting the stage for an exhilarating journey. Traverse epic dungeons, face formidable bosses, wield legendary weapons, and claim abundant loot alongside your companions.

An exciting feature now allows warriors to shift between all classes without limitations, offering a truly versatile experience. World of Kings is set to unveil even more captivating content in its upcoming version, Awakening of the Earth.

Unite with Furries to Save the Eastern Land

As darkness once again casts its shadow over the Eastern lands, the Furries must rally to save their homeland from the clutches of NOX. Heroes, the time has come to unite with the Furries in a quest to reclaim the lost artifact and restore light to the war-torn Eastern land. Warriors, are you ready to embark on this noble expedition?

Exciting Features:

Dungeons

The latest version introduces more than five epic dungeons. Dungeon modes have been simplified, making it easier for players to obtain fantastic loot and gear. Players can now fill roles like tanks, healers, DPS, and work seamlessly as a team. The harder the dungeons, the greater the rewards, making for an exciting adventure.

PvP

The new version brings a slew of adjustments to each class, ensuring that PvP battles are more balanced and competitive. Team up with your friends and venture into the arena and battlefield together.

Engage in duels at your convenience to hone your combat skills. The real-time PvP system ensures that only the strongest can emerge victorious. Take part in massive multiplayer battles, including guild clashes with over 100 players, all fighting for honor and glory.



A Magical World

World of Kings now boasts a new race and additional basic class options. With six distinct races, each carrying its own unique story, and a choice of 10 basic and 30 advanced classes, players have unprecedented flexibility in character creation.

A new level cap is available, granting players new talents upon reaching level 80. The world teems with hundreds of mounts and pets to expand your collection, and its sprawling open world, covering 26 square kilometers, features thousands of NPCs and hidden stories waiting to be uncovered. Discover breathtaking landscapes and explore every corner of this mesmerizing world.

In World of Kings, the stage is set for your grand adventure in a world filled with magic, mystery, and battles of epic proportions. Will you rise to the occasion and become a legend in this captivating MMORPG?
