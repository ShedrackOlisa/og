Torque Burnout

Torque Burnout takes the driving game genre to the next level by combining the best elements of every racing game. Grip the wheel, slam the pedal to the floor, and drive like an absolute maniac, all while performing donuts and drifts like a true champion. Unleash your ride's potential, push it to its absolute limits, electrify the crowd, and dominate the competition to earn the title of Burnout King!


Key Features:

  1. Realistic Burnout Simulation: Torque Burnout delivers a lifelike burnout experience complete with stunning smoke effects, tire explosions, and engines bursting into flames. You'll feel the adrenaline of a true burnout competition.
  2. Diverse Car Selection: Choose from a wide variety of cars, each with its own unique handling and customization options. Select your dream ride and personalize it to match your style.
  3. Thundering Engine Sounds: The game features powerful engine sounds that will send chills down your spine, immersing you in the world of high-performance motors and extreme burnouts.

Torque Burnout allows you to transform into the Burnout King. Get behind the wheel, unleash your inner daredevil, and prove your skills in the ultimate burnout showdown. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a newcomer to the scene, this game offers an electrifying experience that's bound to get your heart racing.

Get ready to experience the COMPACT and enjoy a thrilling burnout adventure. Torque Burnout continues to evolve and expand, delivering endless excitement and new challenges for players.

Game Information and Requirements:

  1. Device Recommendations: To fully enjoy Torque Burnout, it's recommended to use a high-end device from the 2012 model or newer.
  2. Network Connection: While a network connection is recommended, it's not mandatory to play the game. You can still enjoy the adrenaline-pumping action even without an internet connection.
  3. In-App Purchases: Torque Burnout is free to play, but it offers in-game credits that can be purchased using real money through in-app purchases. You can enhance your gameplay experience with these credits.
