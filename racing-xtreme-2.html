Racing Xtreme 2 - Monster Truck

Are you ready to embrace the thundering power of fire-breathing MONSTER TRUCKS and engage in fierce head-to-head racing competitions? The adrenaline-pumping world of extreme monster truck racing is right at your fingertips with Racing Xtreme 2. Get ready to jump behind the wheel of these colossal beasts, set the terrain on fire, race against lightning-fast 4x4 offroad monsters, and experience breathtaking jumps through the picturesque American countryside!


Game Features:

  1. 30 High-Performance MONSTER TRUCKS: Choose from a collection of 30 incredibly powerful monster trucks, each with its unique characteristics.
  2. Stunning 3D Visuals: Immerse yourself in the world of monster truck racing with jaw-dropping 3D graphics that deliver a visually spectacular experience.
  3. Spectacular Jumps and Stunts: Feel the adrenaline rush as you perform jaw-dropping jumps and stunts, conquering the hills and valleys of the American countryside.
  4. Hardcore Challenges: Prove your skills in a variety of hardcore challenges that will test your driving abilities to the limit.
  5. Vehicle Upgrades: Customize your monster truck with upgrades, including new tires, nitrous, exhaust, engine enhancements, gears, boosters, and body modifications.
  6. Head-to-Head Racing: Engage in intense head-to-head racing battles as you drive 4x4 cars and aim to crush your rivals on the track.
  7. Offroad Challenges: Test your truck driving skills on muddy racing tracks that will push your driving abilities to the max.
  8. Online Leaderboards: Compete for the top position on the online leaderboards and establish your dominance in the world of monster trucks.
  9. Various Race Modes: Choose from multiple race modes, including Boss Battle Races, Ranking Races, Limited Races, Extreme Races, Single Contest, and Daily Race mode.

Racing Xtreme 2 invites you to immerse yourself in the world of monster truck mayhem, where you get to choose your favorite powerhouse and unleash it on the track.

You'll experience thrilling racing on beautifully designed terrains filled with valleys, hills, lakes, and railroads. Keep your eyes on the road, your rivals, and the next ramp because you're in for the ride of your life!

It's Time to Race, Speed Up, and Soar:

If you're a fan of monster truck games, truck games, or RC rally games, Racing Xtreme 2 is about to become your favorite. While you aim for peak monster truck performance and speed,

you can also dive into the world of truck customization and design. Here, it's not just about winning races but also crafting the perfect monster truck for your style. Modify your truck's suspension, enhance its tires, and personalize its look to create a true monster on wheels!

Crush Your Rivals and Dominate the Scene:

Racing Xtreme 2 brings the thrill of a Monster Truck Show right to your mobile device. There's no room for being nice in this fast-paced racing simulation. Take the wheel, accelerate to the max, climb the leaderboards, and show your rivals what you're made of.

Make a name for yourself in the world of offroad monster truck racing through sheer speed and strength. Your determination to succeed will be pushed to the limit as you aim to become the ultimate racing champion.



Are you ready to get behind the wheel of your monster truck, customize and upgrade it, and prove your mettle in drag racing and on the finish line? The time for extreme racing has arrived, and it's your moment to shine!

Join the league of extreme racing drivers now and race your way to the top in Racing Xtreme 2. Get ready for a wild ride filled with extreme speed, jaw-dropping stunts, and a surge of adrenaline like no other!
