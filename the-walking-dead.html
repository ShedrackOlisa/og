The Walking Dead: Survivors

Step into the post-apocalyptic world of The Walking Dead and gear up for the newest official strategy game, "The Walking Dead: Survivors."

In this gripping game, your town's survival hinges on your ability to upgrade and fortify your defenses against not only the living but also the relentless undead hordes.

Dive deep into The Walking Dead universe as you assemble a team of iconic comic book characters such as Negan, Rick, and many others.


Battle for Survival

Struggle for survival in the unforgiving realm of The Walking Dead. In this world, every decision you make carries tremendous weight, especially when you cross paths with players from all corners of the globe.

Will you wield your might to dominate, or will you forge alliances and cooperate with others? The power to shape your destiny rests with you.

Game Features

An Official Game

"The Walking Dead: Survivors" is an officially licensed game based on The Walking Dead comic series from Skybound Entertainment.

Immerse yourself in this post-apocalyptic realm where you will recruit and interact with iconic characters such as Rick, Michonne, Negan, Glenn, and more.

Strategic Choices

Every choice you make in "The Walking Dead: Survivors" carries immense consequences, and there's no time for indecision.

Will you channel your efforts into bolstering your defenses and forming alliances, or will you amass a formidable army, embark on conquests, and claim dominion by force?

Tower Defense

Your settlement faces ceaseless threats from approaching walkers, and it falls on your shoulders to fend off the impending doom.

Devise winning strategies to secure victory by fortifying your defenses, setting up barriers, constructing essential structures, enlisting new Survivors, and utilizing their unique skills to keep the walkers at bay.

Social Dynamics

Although walkers pose a dire threat, the world of "The Walking Dead: Survivors" introduces another challenge - interaction with other players worldwide. The decision lies with you as you select your allies and identify your adversaries.

Caution is paramount when placing your trust. Form Clans, erect various clan structures throughout the region, expand your territory, and brace yourself for war against the likes of Negan.



Exploration

"The Walking Dead: Survivors" unveils an expansive regional map teeming with vital locations, characters, resources, and items awaiting discovery.

Familiarizing yourself with your surroundings will prove pivotal to your survival. Traverse the map, pinpoint key structures, and compete with rival clans for control in this quest for supremacy.

Survival in this post-apocalyptic world hinges on your strategy and alliances. Join the ranks of "The Walking Dead: Survivors" and steer your town towards salvation or dominance.
