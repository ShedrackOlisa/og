Top Speed - Drag & Racing!

Top Speed unleashes the thrill of high-octane drag racing, where you'll face off against criminally insane rivals in intense head-to-head drag races. Get ready to dominate the streets and establish your supremacy against fierce criminal competition!


Key Features:

  1. A Diverse Garage: Choose from a jaw-dropping selection of 69 cars, including stock rides, powerful dragsters, and even law enforcement vehicles. Your journey to dominance begins with the car of your choice.
  2. Customization Galore: Let your creative juices flow with an array of tuning and customization options. Upgrade your engine's power, fine-tune your gears, add nitrous boosts, repaint your car, and slap on decals to make your ride truly yours. Your choices will directly impact the realistic driving experience.
  3. Explore 5 City Districts: Immerse yourself in the vibrant world of Top Speed by navigating through five unique city districts, each with its own distinct theme and associated gang crew. From suburban streets to bustling downtown areas, your racing playground knows no bounds.
  4. An Aircraft Carrier Like No Other: Experience the adrenaline rush of racing on an aircraft carrier inspired by real-life designs. This exceptional location adds a new layer of excitement to your drag races.
  5. Arcade Game Modes: Enjoy a variety of thrilling arcade game modes that will put your skills to the test. Whether it's burnouts, asphalt plowing, or unrestricted racing, Top Speed has it all.
  6. Heart-Pounding Police Chases: Feel the rush of adrenaline as you engage in adrenaline-inducing police chases. Evade the authorities and outsmart the cops to secure your victory.
  7. Mind-Blowing 3D HD Visuals: Top Speed offers stunning 3D HD visuals that bring every race to life. The realistic graphics enhance your immersion in the world of drag racing.

Top Speed pushes the boundaries in the drag racing genre, setting a new standard for thrilling gameplay. Are you ready to engage in underground races against unpredictable mafia mobs? Will you dominate the streets by driving luxury cars and showing everyone who's in charge? It's time to take the wheel and prepare to have your breath taken away when you hit that nitro button.

With a roster of 69 cars at your disposal, you'll go head-to-head with 20 criminal overlords, aiming to establish your supremacy in the city. Each car can be meticulously tuned and customized to your liking.

Enhance your engine's power, upgrade your gear and nitro, change the paint job, and add those personal touches with decals. Every modification you make impacts the realism of your driving experience.

When you unlock elite dragsters, you can even access the most hardcore visual mods available in the black market. Every tool from the criminal underworld is yours to utilize; just ensure that you use them wisely in the heat of the race.

Top Speed grants you the freedom to choose from a remarkable selection of 69 cars, ranging from classic stock vehicles to heavily modified dragsters. You can even get behind the wheel of national police vehicles from five different countries. Nothing will stand in your way as you burn rubber on the streets.

All races in Top Speed occur off the grid, away from regular traffic, allowing you to unleash your inner speed demon. Get ready to engage in wild burnouts, tear up the asphalt, and race without limits. As a mafia underdog, you'll have to take on 20 criminal overlords who hold the city in an iron grip.

The city's streets will become your battleground, and ambition and adrenaline will be your guiding forces throughout the race. You might be the newest racing sensation on these streets, but your rivals are yet to discover your full potential. How about proving your skills by leaving them in the dust with a well-timed nitro boost?

Top Speed also offers a tour of five stunning and distinctive city districts. From the serene suburbs to the bustling downtown hub, your desire for diverse scenery will be satisfied.



Seek glory and embrace the adrenaline rush in the Little Asia district, a fusion of Eastern and Western influences. Achieve incredible speeds on the scenic highway. As a professional arcade racer, you'll have ample opportunities to flaunt your ride in beautiful and realistic environments.

Top Speed is your ticket to heart-pounding drag racing adventures. It's where ambition meets the asphalt and adrenaline guides your every move. Get ready to prove your worth on the unforgiving streets and establish yourself as the ultimate ruler of the road. The journey to dominance starts now, so buckle up and let the nitro-fueled action begin!
