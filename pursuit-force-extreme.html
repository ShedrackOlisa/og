Pursuit Force: Extreme Justice (PSP)

Pursuit Force: Extreme Justice invites players to become the commander, the highest-ranking operational officer in a thrilling world of crime-fighting. This action-packed game features four new playable characters, each tasked with a critical mission: to track down and bring to justice five hardcore street gangs operating in and around the notorious Capital City.


Assume the Role of the Commander

In Pursuit Force: Extreme Justice, you take on the role of a commander, the top dog in law enforcement, responsible for orchestrating high-stakes operations to bring justice to the crime-ridden Capital City.

You'll need to use your tactical skills and strategic mind to plan and execute daring missions. As the commander, you're not just a bystander; you're at the forefront of every operation, ensuring that law and order prevail.

The Elite Team of Playable Characters

Extreme Justice introduces a diverse roster of four new playable characters, each with their own unique abilities and backgrounds. These characters bring depth and variety to the gameplay, adding an extra layer of excitement to your missions. Whether you prefer stealthy tactics, sharpshooting skills, or up-close combat, there's a character to suit your style. Get to know them, their stories, and their abilities:

A City in Turmoil

Capital City serves as the backdrop for your high-octane adventures. This sprawling, urban landscape is rife with criminal activity and danger. As a commander, you'll lead your elite team through its gritty streets, skyscrapers, and waterfront, pursuing criminals and restoring peace. Capital City isn't just a setting; it's a living, breathing part of the action that adds depth and atmosphere to the game.

Battling Hardcore Street Gangs

Your mission in Pursuit Force: Extreme Justice is clear: track down and bring to justice five hardcore street gangs that have taken root in Capital City. These gangs are a formidable adversary, each with their own unique characteristics, strategies, and leaders.

Your elite team will need to adapt, strategize, and overcome the challenges posed by these gangs. It's a battle of wits and firepower as you work to dismantle their criminal operations and restore justice.

Conclusion

Pursuit Force: Extreme Justice is not your typical action game. It offers an immersive experience where you take on the role of a commander, lead an elite team of characters, and engage in thrilling missions to combat hardcore street gangs.

Capital City is your playground, and justice is your goal. Get ready to unleash your inner commander and dive into a world of action, strategy, and adventure. The fate of the city rests in your hands.
